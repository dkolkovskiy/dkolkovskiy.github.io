CyberSun Confidential Information Policy

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Confidential Information Policy is to protect the sensitive and proprietary information of CyberSun. This policy outlines the standards for handling, accessing, and sharing confidential information to ensure its security and integrity.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who have access to CyberSun's confidential information.

3. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Business strategies and plans
  • Financial records and projections
  • Product designs and technical specifications
  • Source code and software documentation
  • Customer and vendor information
  • Personnel records
  • Any non-public information that, if disclosed, could harm the interests of CyberSun

4. Access to Confidential Information

  • Role-Based Access: Access to confidential information will be granted based on the individual's role and responsibilities within the company.
  • Authorization: Employees must obtain authorization from their department head to access specific types of confidential information not directly related to their job functions.

5. Handling and Protection

  • Data Encryption: All electronic confidential information must be encrypted using company-approved encryption methods.
  • Physical Security: Confidential documents in physical form should be stored in locked cabinets or secure areas accessible only to authorized personnel.
  • Digital Security: Secure workstations, regularly update passwords, and use multi-factor authentication where possible.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

  • Controlled Distribution: Confidential information should only be shared with individuals who have a legitimate need to know for business purposes.
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs): Third-parties and new employees must sign an NDA before being granted access to any confidential information.

7. Reporting and Breaches

  • Incident Reporting: Any suspected or actual breach of this policy must be reported immediately to the IT Security department.
  • Investigation and Response: The company will promptly investigate any reported breaches of confidentiality and take appropriate corrective actions.

8. Enforcement

Violation of this Confidential Information Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contracts, as well as potential legal action.

9. Review and Updates

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure it remains relevant and effective in protecting CyberSun's confidential information.

10. Contact Information

For questions regarding this policy or to report a breach, please contact the IT Security department at info@cybersun.com
